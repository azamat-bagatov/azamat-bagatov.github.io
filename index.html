About the Author

Les Podervianskyi - an iconic representative of the Ukrainian underground, born in a renowned family of artists, mastermind behind the “Club of Aesthetic Disobedience”, an “Aesthet on the Barricade”, a “Classic from the Catacombs”, a womanizer, a painter, a playwright, a poet, a tall handsome man with an aristocratic last name, Ukraine’s chief swearer, a traveller, a romantic, a person of art… Talking about bright personalities is easy - you can go on forever. For my part, I can add and call him by that untranslatable but deeply meaningful Ukrainian word – Mytets’ – The Artist. He himself calls himself a painter.

Yuri Yefimov, a friend
